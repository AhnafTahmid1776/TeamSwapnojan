About Team Swapnojan

Team Swapnojan is a Formula Student Team from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology . We are a dedicated group of students working to build a Formula student car with great dedication & the motive to fulfill our dreams. The team, which consists of more than 25 undergraduate students from various academic majors, is attempting to build a Formula Student racecar for upcoming Formula student competitions in 2022. As a team, we have quite a few legacies to our name. In 2017, we took part in “ISEI India” and won two awards and also, in 2021, we competed in “Formula Bharat 2021” and won two awards as well. Here is a short glimpse of our achievements so far, !

What is Formula Student?

‘Formula Student (FS) is Europe’s most established educational motorsport competition, run by the Institution of Mechanical Engineers. Backed by industry and high profile engineers such as Ross Brawn OBE, the competitin aims to inspire and develop enterprising and innovative young engineers. Universities from across the globe are challenged to design and build a single-seat racing car in order to compete in static and dynamic events, which demonstrate their understanding and test the performance of the vehicle.’ -via the IMechE

Our future Goal

Our achievements have always been our fuel of motivation. Following our success of 2021 in Formula Bharat, We have taken even bigger goals for 2022 to participate in Regional countries to challenge our ability & push ourselves to our absolute limits
